How to add skinnablity to your VB project

Now it's very simple to make your VB project skinnable. First Add mdul_Main.bas to your project. You can delete the ShowTutorial procedure, it's not needed. Then add following lines of code to your form's Load event:

Private Sub Form_Load()
   Call Initialize
End Sub

That's it. Simply use: Call ChangeSkinTo[skin name], then it's done. For example:

Call ChangeSkinToTitanium
